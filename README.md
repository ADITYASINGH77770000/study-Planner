## 💡 What is Study Coach?

**Study Coach** is a fully AI-powered exam preparation platform. Give it your **mood**, **time left**, and **notes** — it builds a complete **day-by-day study roadmap** with exam topic probabilities, a formula sheet, curated resources and mood-matched music. No generic plans. Every plan is built for YOU, right now.

## 🎭 Mood System

| Mood | Strategy | Best For |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 😴 Exhausted | Light revision only | When you're burnt out |
| 😤 Stressed | Easiest topics first | Pre-exam anxiety days |
| 😐 Meh | Pomodoro bursts | Low motivation days |
| 😊 Good | Focused deep work | Regular study days |
| ⚡ Energized | Hardest topics first | Peak performance days |
